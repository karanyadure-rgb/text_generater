1. **Research and Development**: Gen AI Foundation supports cutting-edge research in AI, with a focus on areas like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
2. **Education and Training**: The foundation provides training programs, workshops, and online courses to equip individuals with the necessary skills to work with AI.
3. **Industry Collaboration**: Gen AI Foundation facilitates partnerships between industry leaders, startups, and academia to foster innovation and commercialization of AI solutions.
4. **Ethics and Governance**: The foundation promotes responsible AI development and deployment, with a focus on ethics, transparency, and accountability.
